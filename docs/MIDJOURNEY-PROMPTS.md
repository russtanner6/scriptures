# Midjourney Prompts for Scripture Book Images

Every prompt uses the style of Yongsung Kim — a Korean LDS artist known for his luminous, reverent depictions of Christ and biblical scenes with dramatic lighting, rich warm tones, and photorealistic oil painting quality.

**Format:** 4:1 landscape ratio images for the Scripture Reader header. Each image should be atmospheric, cinematic, and suitable as a wide banner.

**Usage:** Copy the prompt into Midjourney. Add `--ar 4:1` for the landscape banner ratio, or `--ar 16:9` for a wider crop option.

---
